6 - EXT. DERELICT BUILDING - DAY <<COLOUR SEQUENCE>>

A Jaguar bumps across some railroad tracks and approaches a large abandoned EXT. DERELICT BUILDING. Leonard is driving. He wears a BEIGE SUIT and BLUE SHIRT (no blood). Next to him is TEDDY. Leonard stops the Jaguar next to a PICKUP TRUCK.

LEONARD

Looks like somebody’s home.

TEDDY

That thing's been here for years.

LEONARD

What are you talking about?

These tracks are only a few days old.

TEDDY

Tracks? What are you? Pocahontas?

Come on!

LEONARD

Let's take a look inside.

7 - INT. DERELICT BUILDING - DAY <<COLOUR SEQUENCE>>

Leonard pulls a stack of POLAROID PHOTOGRAPHS out of his pocket and leafs through them. He finds a photo showing Teddy standing in front of the pickup truck. On the white strip beneath the photo is handwritten: “TEDDY GAMMELL TEL. 555 0134”. Behind he reads:

LEONARD (V.O.)

"Teddy.

Don't believe his lies.

He is the one. Kill him."

I finally found him.

How long have I been looking?

Leonard stuffs the Polaroids back into his pocket and draws a HANDGUN.

TEDDY

Find anything? Didn't think so.

Let's go. Fuck this.

Teddy turns and heads for the door. Leonard leaps on him, pistol-whipping him furiously as he shouts:

LEONARD

You're gonna pay for what you did.

Beg forgiveness, and then you pay.

TEDDY

Leonard.

You don't have a clue, you freak.

LEONARD

Beg my wife's forgiveness

before I blow your brains out.

TEDDY

Leonard, you don't know what's going on.

You don't even know my name.
